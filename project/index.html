Simple, Reliable Streaming

Welcome to The Webcast Project, a custom-built streaming solution designed to replace the Teradek system traditionally used by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. With a focus on simplicity, affordability, and flexibility, Webcast enables anyone to set up a reliable livestream using a Raspberry Pi and our custom software - no complicated configuration required.

Why I Built Webcast

I was looking for a straightforward, easy-to-use system that could be used to stream Sunday services during COVID. After spending some time and not finding anything that fit my needs, I decided to build it myself!

Features at a Glance

  • Preconfigured Image: Download and flash the Webcast image to your Raspberry Pi. No complex software installation required.
  • Customizable Streaming: Choose to stream directly to the Church webcast system, or any RTMP-compatible service, including YouTube, Facebook, or Twitch;
  • Network Flexibility: Works seamlessly on both wired and wireless connections, making it ideal for both fixed and temporary locations.
  • Low Bandwidth Friendly: Optimize streaming for locations with limited upload speeds, ensuring smooth deliver even at 500kbps.
  • Full Control: Start and stop streams directly from your browser or our custom app, or control the device remotely with our power management setup.
