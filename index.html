Colorado, USA

Colorado is a state located in the western United States. It is known for its diverse geography, which includes alpine mountains, high plains, deserts with huge sand dunes, and deep canyons. The state is home to over 5.7 million people and is the 21st most populous state in the United States. Colorado is known for its outdoor recreation, with many opportunities for skiing, snowboarding, hiking, and camping. The state is also home to many national parks and monuments, including Rocky Mountain National Park, Mesa Verde National Park, and the Great Sand Dunes National Park and Preserve.
